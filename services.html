Sliding Doors

Magnador’s steel sliding driveway gates are hot dipped galvanised and are custom made to your individual requirements providing you with the exact sliding gates you require. They are then powder coated with an exterior Epoxy-Polyester powder coating, giving the product a scratch and fade resistant finish. Various designs and colours are available to choose from. These steel security gates are engineered to last! We offer a range of sliding gate options and styles to suit our clients’ taste, and if requested,

AUTOMATION

The gate motors of our steel driveway gates are fitted by qualified technicians only. Various size motors are available depending on application, from domestic to industrial gate motors. A battery backup is fitted to ensure the motor will function during a power failure.
